Show
1. Track your walk, run, cycle, treadmill, or gym distance in Strava, Garmin, Apple Fitness, Samsung Health, or any fitness app.

2. Log your distance inside this app.

3. Start with the 10K tier. When you hit it, you can send proof or continue to 21K.

4. Hit 21K and you can send proof again or push on to the full 42K marathon.

5. Collectable cards unlock across the full marathon path, with the final card at 41K. The belt unlocks at 42K.
